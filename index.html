Features

Productivity at its best

Axis is packed with features that help you stay productive and focused. Browsers should be tools that help you get things done, not distractions that keep you from your work.

Persistent Tabs

Your tabs stay exactly where you left them, even after closing and reopening the browser.

Instant Theme Switching

Switch between light and dark themes instantly with a single click.

Spotlight Search

Quickly find and navigate to your tabs, bookmarks, and history with powerful search.

Customizable Sidebar

Organize your browsing with a fully customizable sidebar that adapts to your workflow.
